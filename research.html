Research Areas

Our laboratory focuses on cutting-edge research in DNA molecular storage and DNA nanotechnology, dedicated to exploring the potential of DNA as an information storage medium and developing innovative storage technologies based on DNA nanostructures.

DNA Data Storage Systems

DNA molecules offer advantages as information storage media, including high storage density, excellent stability, and low energy consumption. We are committed to developing efficient and reliable DNA data storage systems, researching end-to-end technologies from encoding algorithms to information retrieval.

  • Development of extended alphabet DNA storage systems based on 5-methylcytosine to increase information storage density
  • Design of efficient DNA encoding algorithms to optimize data conversion efficiency
  • Research on DNA Bloom Filter technology for contamination resistance and file version control
  • Development of DNA data reading technology based on nanopore sequencing

Applications of DNA Nanotechnology in Storage

We study the application of DNA nanostructures in information storage, enhancing storage density and reading efficiency through precise design of DNA molecular spatial structures, advancing the practical development of DNA storage technology.

  • Development of high-density information encoding technology based on DNA origami
  • Research on stability and controllability of DNA nanostructures
  • Development of rapid information retrieval systems based on DNA nanotechnology
  • Exploration of DNA nanostructures in long-term storage applications
