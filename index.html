DNA Storage and DNA Nanotechnology

Welcome to the DNA Storage Lab. Our research is dedicated to DNA molecular storage and DNA nanotechnology for storage applications.

We develop DNA data storage systems and explore innovative DNA nanotechnology to address key challenges in information storage, specifically focusing on DNA nanostructure design and high-density information encoding.

Our goal is to unlock the full potential of DNA molecules for efficient and stable data storage, particularly by leveraging DNA nanotechnology to overcome limitations in storage density and readout efficiency. Through interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation, we aim to drive the practical advancement of DNA storage technologies.

Research Areas

DNA Data Storage

DNA data storage systems are under development, including efficient encoding, error correction, and retrieval methods. Research prioritizes storage density, read accuracy, and stability, alongside the creation of new DNA synthesis and sequencing techniques for improved data storage and access efficiency.

DNA Nanotechnology

The application of DNA nanostructures for information storage is being explored, leveraging techniques like DNA origami and DNA bricks. This research aims to improve storage density and readout efficiency via designed DNA nanostructures, and to unlock their potential for information encoding and data security.
